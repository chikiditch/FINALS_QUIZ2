88FRYER

Best Gig place

88 FRYER is a live music venue located at the ITC Building, Panay Avenue corner Timog Avenue, Quezon City, Philippines. The venue is known for hosting various live performances and events. 88Fryer is not your ordinary pub—it’s a one-of-a-kind sanctuary that pays homage to the incredible talents of OPM artists.


It has established itself as a vibrant spot for live performances, offering a platform for both emerging and established artists across various music genres. The venue is known for its engaging atmosphere, combining live music with a selection of beverages and food. While specific details about its founding date and early history are not readily available, the first post on the place was recorded on August 1 2022 via their Facebook porfile.


Our band "Lightbringer" has been performing at 88Fryer for the past 11 months. We have had the pleasure of sharing the stage with some of the most talented musicians in the country. The venue has become a second home for us, and we are grateful for the opportunity to perform in front of such a supportive audience. Also, the venue has a great sound system and lighting setup, which makes it a pleasure to perform there.
